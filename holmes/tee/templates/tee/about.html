-Our Mission-

To provide quality education from the basic level to secondary level. we collaborate with their parents and staff, focusing on the development of all dimensions of their personality, intellectual, character, spiritual, physical and social. The education is given in accordance with christian principles and in an atmosphere of freedom, responsibility and trust.

Vision

To produce responsible and outstanding 21st century students, who would help develop and improve nigeria and the world

Our Values

Hard work, sense of commitment, upright moral living, team spirit, concern and care for all, respect, freedom, sincerity and responsibility. Our style of educating permits a wide variety of pedagogical methods so long as they are coherent with the philosophy of the school
